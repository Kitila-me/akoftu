About Akoftu

Akoftu is an Ethiopian coffee company based out of the capital Addis Ababa and Sebeta town in Oromia region. Our company focuses on sourcing, storing and exporting green coffee beans from all over the coffee belt in the Ethiopian highlands. We have partners ranging from hundreds of small-scale coffee farmers and local traders to farmers unions and large-scale coffee traders with whom we have been working to source Ethiopian coffee of the highest grades. We work with many farmers in the Jimma and Wollega areas where two of the best tasting coffee varieties (Limmu and Lekempti) are grown to ensure the coffee we export is always of the highest quality and is delivered on time.
